Lower Mainland and Vancouver Warehouse

Green Light Group's extensive network of more than 95,000 square feet of warehouse space provides customers with a large storage capacity available at short notice and altered to suit demand. This flexibility is essential for businesses facing volatile market conditions or experiencing declining or rapid growth.

We pride ourselves on working closely with clients, to best fulfill and exceed their expectations. We are able to integrate with our clients operations, offering an individually tailored service.

Our strength lies in the capability of our people and our ability to react quickly in partnership with our customers. We take time to understand your business and organize ourselves around your requirements. Adding value to our customers businesses is the foundation of what we do and is what has placed us at the forefront of our industry.

We provide safe secure warehousing with all products controlled in our warehouse management system that allows our clients to view their inventory 24/7.

We offer multiple services ranging from:

  • Providing Long & Short Term Storage
  • Handling of Project Cargo and Exhibition Events
  • Export & Import Consolidation / De-Consolidation
  • Cross-Dock Service
  • Automobile loading & unloading
  • Pick n Pack
  • Distribution
  • Sufferance Bond Cargo
  • CBSA Custom Examination

We deliver what you need, when you need it, and where you need it! The friendship of those we serve is the foundation of our progress; Green Light Group is your one stop solution for all your warehousing and logistics needs.
