Brand Direction

Our brand uses a strict combination of high-contrast structure (Grey) and vibrant interaction cues (Green).

Typography Hierarchies

We separate structural headings from readable body content to ensure clarity.

Header (Open Sans Bold)

Used for page titles, section headers, and navigation items. Communicates structure.

Body Copy (Arial)

Used for detailed descriptions, product listings, and articles. Optimized for readability.

Color Application

Colors are strictly assigned to specific roles to communicate function:
