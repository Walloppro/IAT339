For the past 15 years, the Green Light Group has been successfully providing various modes of transportation as a third party link in our customer's supply chain requirements throughout BC, Canada, and cross-border to/from USA & Mexico. These services include local pickup and delivery, full load (FTL), less than full load (LTL), Drayage between all major ports & rail terminals in the Low Mainland and as far reaching as Alberta and beyond.

Green Light is proud to be a 100% BC owned and operated company, with professional and dedicated drivers, office & warehouse staff ready to provide service to our customers with pride and confidence. Our success is built on our unique ability to adapt to any circumstance 24 hours a day, seven days a week. Our customers know when they have a problem, we will find a way to help to resolve and meet their needs.

Our fleet

Utilizing our fleet of straight trucks equipped with power trailgates, allows us to deliver to residences, retail stores and malls.

With over 20 tractors, we offer On-Time appointment container drayage to / from all local ports, ferry and rail terminals.

Whether it's frozen or perishable, Green Light Group has the expertise to have your shipment arrive on time and at the proper temperature.

Our Flat Bed Division provides specialized equipment to handle over-sized commodities such as machinery, lumber, aircraft engines, etc.

Our portfolio of intermodal services offer a range of cost-effective, streamlined services that provide the flexibility to control and manage your good and improve your bottom line.
